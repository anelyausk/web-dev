Recipe #1: Easy Chicken Piccata

  • Time: 25 mins
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
chicken breasts 2
flour 1 cup
butter 1/2 stick
black pepper to taste

Step 1: Slice chicken breasts horizontally to yield 4 pieces. Place breasts between 2 sheets of plastic wrap and pound to 1/4-inch thin. Place flour on a plate and dredge each breast on both sides in flour.

Step 2:Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Put chicken into the skillet and season tops with salt and pepper. Turn when nicely browned, about 4 minutes, and season again. Cook second side until browned, about 4 minutes. Transfer breasts to a plate, reserving skillet drippings.

Step 3:Add wine and lemon juice to the skillet, stirring to scrape up any browned bits. Simmer to reduce and thicken sauce, about 5 minutes more. Add capers and pour sauce over chicken breasts on the plate. Sprinkle with parsley.

Recipe #2: Kale Banana Smoothie

  • Time: 10 mins
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
coconut water 16
banana 1
avocado 1/2
kale 1/2 cup

Step 1: Put coconut water, banana, avocado, kale, lemon juice, and cayenne pepper in blender; blend until smooth, about 30 seconds.

Recipe #3: Lighter Baked Shrimp Scampi

  • Time: 25 mins
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
shrimp 16
butter 1 tablespoon
garlic 2 tablespoon
shallot 1 tablespoon

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Place shrimp in a shallow baking dish.

Step 2:Melt butter in a small saucepan over medium heat. Add garlic, shallot, and olive oil and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute. Stir in chicken stock, lemon juice, red pepper flakes, paprika, salt, and pepper. Pour this mixture over shrimp and sprinkle with crushed croutons.
